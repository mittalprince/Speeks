Why Public Speaking is Importnant
Speaking in front of others is a big challenge for most of the individuals.We often find the youth lacks the confidence to express themselves in front of others, as they hesitate and have a fear of speaking and lack of confidence, which is a huge concern because when it comes to cooperative world, one is supposed to be expressive and confident about their opinions and thoughts. Stage fear is one of the deadliest fear among the youth, thus in order to tackle the same, we have come up with a solution of giving them their personal space and providing them a platform to improve and nurture themselves.
How Speeks Helps
We build an application which revolve around solving the public speaking problems and act as personal public speaking analyser which detect your flaws and help you improve your public speaking/speaking skills. This will help an individual in self analysis and boost their confidence as they practice with it and learn to be more expressive.
The analysis of the content would be done in the following parameters :
  1. Grammar check
  2. Speech emotion recognition
  3. Confidence level
  4. Expression detection.
